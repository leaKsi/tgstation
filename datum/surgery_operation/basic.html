basic
/datum/surgery_operation/basic

Basic operations are a simple base type for surgeries that

  1. Target a specific zone on humans
  2. Work on non-humans

Use this as a bsae if your surgery needs to work on everyone

"operating_on" is the mob being operated on, be it carbon or non-carbon. If the mob is carbon, we check the relevant bodypart for surgery states and traits. No bodypart, no operation. If the mob is non-carbon, we just check the mob directly.
